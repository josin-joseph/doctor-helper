Disease: idiopathic excessive menstruation

Common symptoms include:
sharp abdominal pain, involuntary urination, vaginal discharge, intermenstrual bleeding, cramps and spasms, blood clots during menstrual periods, long menstrual periods, heavy menstrual flow, unpredictable menstruation, painful menstruation, frequent menstruation

This information is for medical education purposes.
Consult a healthcare professional for diagnosis and treatment.
